# Privacy Policy — Realtors Buddy

**Last updated: June 18, 2026**

Realtors Buddy ("the app", "we", "us") is provided by Tyler Grieve. This policy
explains what information the app does and does not handle. Contact:
**itygrmedia@gmail.com**.

---

## The short version
- The information you enter — listings, commissions, leads, contacts, dates, and
  notes — is **stored only on your device**. We do not collect it, upload it, see
  it, or sell it.
- Backups are created **only when you choose to export them**, and they are saved
  to a location **you** pick (such as your own iCloud Drive or Files).
- The app shows ads through **Google AdMob**. To serve ads, Google may collect
  certain device and usage information as described below. You can limit this when
  the app asks for tracking permission.

---

## Information you enter (stored only on your device)
All real estate data you create in the app — property listings, commission
figures, transaction checklists, lead names, phone numbers, emails, budgets,
follow-up dates, and notes — is saved locally on your device using Apple's Core
Data. This information:

- is **not transmitted to us or any server we control**,
- is **not collected, analyzed, or sold** by us, and
- remains on your device until you delete it or delete the app.

**Backups & exports.** When you tap Export or Backup in Settings, the app creates
a file (JSON or CSV) and lets you save it where you choose — for example your own
iCloud Drive or Files. Those copies are controlled entirely by you and are subject
to the terms of whatever storage service you select. We never receive them.

**Deleting your data.** Because your data lives only on your device, deleting the
app removes it. Export a backup first if you want to keep it.

## Information collected automatically (advertising)
The app displays advertising provided by **Google AdMob**. To deliver and measure
ads, Google and its partners may automatically collect information such as:

- a device advertising identifier,
- IP address and approximate location derived from it,
- device type, operating system, and app interaction/usage data.

This collection is governed by Google's policies, not ours:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- How Google uses information from sites/apps that use its services:
  https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

We do not receive or store the data AdMob collects.

---

## Your choices — App Tracking Transparency
When required, the app asks for your permission before allowing tracking across
other companies' apps and websites (Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt). If
you decline, you can still use the app and you will still see ads, but they may be
less relevant (non-personalized). You can change this anytime in
**iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking**.

---

## Children
The app is intended for real estate professionals and is **not directed to
children under 13** (or the equivalent minimum age in your region). We do not
knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Third-party services
- **Google AdMob** — advertising (see links above).
- **Apple** — app distribution and, if you choose to store backups there, iCloud
  Drive/Files. Apple's privacy policy: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

## Security
Your in-app data is stored within the app's private storage on your device and is
protected by your device's security (passcode/Face ID/Touch ID and iOS app
sandboxing). No method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and any backups you
export are your responsibility to protect.

## Changes to this policy
We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will
change when we do. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the
revised policy.
